How to Use Signify

Getting Started

Signify is a web-based platform for secure digital signature verification. To get started, simply navigate through our website using the menu above.

Adding Signatures

Click the "Add Signature" button on the home page to begin the process of adding a digital signature to your documents. Our secure system ensures your signature is protected with enterprise-grade encryption.

Verifying Signatures

Use the "Verify Signature" button to authenticate existing digital signatures. Our advanced fraud detection intelligence helps identify potential security risks and ensures document integrity.
